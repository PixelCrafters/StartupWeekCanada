StartupWeek is Coming to Canada

The original StartupWeek was created by Andrew Hyde in Boulder, CO. Andrew has rejoined UP Global, the non-profit behind Startup Weekend, to roll out Startup Week around the world. Read more about UP Global / Startup Week here »

Here on the northern side of the maple syrup curtain, we're organizing to start running Startup Week events as well. We hope to secure some national sponsors and help UP Global build the template for events here. Thinking of running a Startup Week in Canada? Please add (even tentative!) dates to Lanyrd, and we'll include them in the guide below »
